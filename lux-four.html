AC Four Bed Deluxe at Hotel Mayur Haridwar is a Four bedded luxury rooms with two full size double beds or twin beds, seems to be the perfect set of Luxury on budget for an easy accommodation of four persons or a Family. These extra spacious big & bright rooms are Air conditioned with LED TV, sofa seating arrangement, in-room wardrobe & safe, clean attached bath & washroom, intercom phone and open-able windows with Private attached balcony for a picturesque mountainous & hilly scenic view.

Comfortable and luxurious rooms with all the classic facilities and round-the-clock room service can be availed along with a delighted stay by opting for AC Four Bed Super Deluxe(Luxury) at Hotel Mayur, Haridwar.
