DOUBLE BED LUXURY ROOM

AC Double Bed Rooms at Hotel Mayur Haridwar are Spacious in its layout, this elegant room is designed with sleek tile floors and is well-furnished with a writing desk, wardrobe for clothing & luggage, a dining table for two people, and a Queen-size bed & attached bathroom. The living space in the bedroom features a sofa set, a telephone with direct dial access and a Color television are also available in this accommodation.

This double bedded room is cream-colored and maintained with open-able window which offers excellent city view.
